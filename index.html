    Day1: Children and social media

    Kara is a tech journalist, a podcast host, and a monther of four. She wrote a popular book called "Burn Book: A tech Love Story". She talks about a new measure that says children under 14 can't have social media without permission. According to Kara, social media can be addictive just like cigarettes, and they aren't good for young people. It is important to understand how soical media affects them, especially girls. The govenrment must make rules about social media. She expresses concerns about the addictive nature of social media and its impact on adolescent mental health. She also talks about the ongoing debate about TikTok and why some people want to ban it in the US. The government needs to take more proactive regulatory measures regarding foreign-owned tech platforms in the country. She ended the interview by saying she hopes the future will be more like Star Trek than Star Wars.
